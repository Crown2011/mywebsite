Amelia The Dreamer: Amelia and the Golden Key

The night before her eighth birthday, Amelia's world changes forever.

She lives in Willow’s Peak, a quiet town wrapped in mist, stars, and old forests no one dares to enter.
With her warm-hearted grandmother, Mama J, her best friend Benji, and memories of her Aunt
who vanished without explanation, Amelia's life feels ordinary until dreams begin to call her by name.

A mysterious golden key.
A star-marked toy named Lulu.
A boy by a moonlit lake who feels strangely familiar.

As Amelia's dreams grow brighter and stranger, she begins to sense a hidden realm beyond sleep.
One where stars whisper, forests remember, and courage is born from kindness. But not everyone is ready to cross into that world… and neither is she.

Tender, magical, and filled with quiet wonder, Amelia the Dreamer: Amelia and the Glowing Key is the beginning of a middle-grade fantasy series about belonging, bravery, and the power of believing in your own light.

✨ Perfect for readers who love gentle fantasy, dream worlds, and magical friendships
✨ A cozy, emotionally rich story for ages 8–12
✨ The first book in an enchanting new series
